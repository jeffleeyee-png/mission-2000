# SupplyChain Hunter

Goal: Whenever a startup is found, estimate whether the company needs Shenzhen.

Use this only for startups with a real or likely hardware product.

Check whether the product likely needs:

- Display
- PCB
- Assembly
- Battery
- Injection
- Certification
- Bluetooth
- Sensors

If Shenzhen suppliers likely exist, output the manufacturing opportunity.

Rank by:

Supply Chain Probability x Jeff Opportunity Score

## Manufacturing Path Checklist

- Prototype
- EVT
- DVT
- PVT
- Certification
- Pilot run
- Mass production

## Shenzhen Fit Signals

- Needs custom electronics
- Needs enclosure or injection molding
- Needs battery sourcing
- Needs display module sourcing
- Needs sensor sourcing
- Needs Bluetooth/Wi-Fi module integration
- Needs assembly partner
- Needs certification support
- Has funding but small team
- Recently launched or preparing preorder
