# Opportunity Hunter

Goal: Every day find only opportunities that Jeff can execute.

Reject anything that cannot realistically generate revenue within 30 days.

Rank by:

Expected Revenue x Probability x Jeff Match

Output only Top 5.

## Filters

- Revenue possible within 30 days: required
- Jeff can execute without a team: required
- No dashboard: required
- No frontend: required
- No database: required
- Markdown only: required
